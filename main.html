Why I want to become a developer?

Creative Problem Solving: Developers solve complex, real-world problems. If you enjoy tackling challenges and finding efficient solutions, this is a great way to put that into action. High Demand and Career Stability: Developers are in high demand, and tech skills are needed in almost every industry. This demand can lead to a rewarding, stable career path. Continuous Learning and Growth: Technology evolves constantly, so there’s always something new to learn. This can be motivating if you’re naturally curious and want to keep growing. Flexibility and Remote Opportunities: Many developer roles offer remote work and flexible hours. If work-life balance or location independence is a priority, a development career can support that. Building Something Valuable: Developers create tools, applications, and solutions that help people. There’s a lot of fulfillment in building something others find useful. Good Earning Potential: Developer roles tend to offer competitive salaries, especially as you gain experience or specialize in high-demand areas.
